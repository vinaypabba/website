Frequently Asked Question
Your inquiries, fully addressed.

How Long Does Grooming Take?

Grooming times vary, typically 3-4 hours, shorter for smaller breeds

Do you use drying cages?

We prioritize safety and well-being—no drying cages, all paws pampered by hand.

Why does pet grooming take so long?

Groomers prioritize safety and comfort, taking time with nervous dogs, allowing breaks.

Do all dogs need grooming?

Essential grooming varies; some dogs need more care for health and welfare.

Can I stay and watch while you groom my pet?

Pet's love can lead to wiggling during grooming, impacting safety.

I need to bring anything with my pooch for their treatment?

Prep tip: Allow pets to relieve themselves before the spa; consider meals wisely.

How often should my pet be groomed?

Grooming frequency depends on your pet's coat; find details in our schedule.

What type of payment do you accept?

We welcome cash, debit cards (no fee), and credit cards (with a £1 surcharge).
